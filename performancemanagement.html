Performance management and People development

“Performance Management is a holistic management discipline which needs to connect many relevant dots to involve development, enablement, and enhancement.” ― Pearl Zhu

Using a performance management system, you can create benchmarks for each employee's talent development journey.

What we do

Coach the leadership team for high- performance. As leadership starts with self- awareness we use the The MBTI® assessment which helps individuals understand their strengths, their preferred working styles, and ultimately helps them see their potential.

How we work

We discuss the organizational current/desire state on performance and define the necessary steps to improve the performance. We facilitate The MBTI Type indicator session which is used in leadership training to achieve concrete results. We facilitate the session at the location of your organization. There is a certain preparation taking place prior to the MBTI session. We will plan the session together and follow up on identified development opportunities, if required. On top, we provide workshop on giving and receiving constructive feedback.
