Change Management

"Change your life today. Don't gamble on the future, act now, without delay!" - Simone de Beauvoir

Organization are continuously changing. When an organization engages in a new change, this impacts people and processes and the change needs to be approached thoughtfully. At the end of the day, the success will depend on the motivation and willingness of the people who engage themselves in the new way of doing business.

What we do

We prepare for the change. The first step is understanding the current situation but even more important, designing the desired future. We will take the leaders in a journey of defining the different steps of this change journey.

How we work

We start by identifying the change(size/scope/time). Consequently, we identify the stakeholders, build a clear communication plan and proceed with the rest of the actions. We provide “change awareness” training for people involved in the change journey. The larger goal is to increase awareness of our personal reaction to change and find the best way how to consciously respond to it. We facilitate The MBTI Type indicator session which is used in leadership training to achieve concrete results. We start with MBTI assessment and build further.

We believe that...

Change takes time, and yet we want it now. Change journey can not be prescribed, and yet processes and clear plans are needed to take an organization from A to B. While change remains a choice, results emerge only when objectives are clearly defined. While the future is still to be determined, only a clear vision of it and a playing to win culture can make all involved people thrive.
