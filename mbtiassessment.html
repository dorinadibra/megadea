MBTI Session Facilitation

Megadea is a nationally recognized management consulting firm that, over the past 25 years, has surveyed and trained a quarter of a million managers and employees across a broad range of industries.

The MBTI® assessment helps individuals understand their strengths, their preferred working styles, and ultimately helps them see their potential.

What we do

We facilitate The MBTI Type indicator session which is used in leadership training to achieve concrete results. We start with MBTI assessment and build further.

How we work

We facilitate the session at the location of your organization. There is certain preparation taking place prior to the face to face session. We will plan the session and potential follow up on identified development opportunities.
